## ScalaMock

## Tips
- https://scalamock.org/user-guide/faq/

- `final` and `private` cannot be mocked
- `val` and `lazy val` cannot be mocked
- `object` cannot be mocked
- `hashcode`, `equals`, `clone` or `toString` cannot be mocked
