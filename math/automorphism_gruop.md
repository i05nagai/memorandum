## Automorphism group


## Example1
- $V$
    - linear space

An automorphism of $V$ is an invertible linear map from $V$ to $V$.

$$
    \mathrm{Aut}(V)
    :=
    \{
        f: V \rigtharrow V
        \mid
        f
        \text{ is an invertible inear map}
    \}
    .
$$
