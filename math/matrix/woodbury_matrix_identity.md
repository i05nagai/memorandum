## Woodbury Matrix Identity
Formula is also known ad Woodbury formula.



## Sherman-Morrison formula
This formula is a special case of Woodbury formula


#### Theorem
* $A \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}$,

$$
\begin{equation}
    (A + uv^{\mathrm{T}})^{-1}
    =
    A^{-1}
    -
    \frac{
        A^{-1}uv^{\mathrm{T}}A^{-1}
    }{
        1
        +
        v^{\mathrm{T}}A^{-1}u
    }
\end{equation}
    .
$$

#### proof
