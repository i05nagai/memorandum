## Bernoulli Distribution
Discrete distribution.

* $p \in [0, 1]$,

$\mathrm{Ber}(x; p)$ is the p.d.f of Bernoulli distribution given $p$.

$$
    x \in \{0, 1\},
    \
    \mathrm{Ber}(x; p)
    :=
    \begin{cases}
        p
        &
            x = 1 \\
        1 - p
        &
            x = 0
    \end{cases}
$$
