## Simpson rule
3-points Newton-Cotes formulas.

##

$$
\begin{eqnarray}
    \int_{a}^{b}
        f(x)
    \ dx
    & \approx &
        \frac{
            \delta x
        }{
            3
        }
        \left(
            f(x_{0})
            +
            4
            f(x_{1})
            +
            2
            f(x_{2})
            +
            4
            f(x_{3})
            +
            2
            f(x_{4})
            +
            \cdots
            +
            4
            f(x_{n-1})
            +
            2
            f(x_{n})
        \right)
    .
    \nonumber
\end{eqnarray}
$$
