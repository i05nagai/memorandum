## Gamma Function

#### Definition
* $z \in \mathbb{C}$,
* $\mathrm{Re}(z) > -1$,

$$
    z \in \mathbb{C},
    \
    \mathrm{Re}(z) > -1,
    \
    \Gamma(z + 1)
    :=
    \int_{0}^{\infty}
        t^{z}
        e^{-t}
    \ dt
    .
$$
