## 01-04. Natural Transformations

#### Definition natural transformations
- $S, T: C \rightarrow B$,
    - functors
- $\tau: S \dot{\rightarrow} T$,
    $\tau_{c} := \tau c: Sc \rightarrow Tc$

$\tau$ is said to be natural transformation if the diagram is 

$$
\begin{array}{ccccc}
    c
    &
    & S_{c}
    & \rightarrow
    & Tc
    \\
    \downarrow f
    &
    & \downarrow Sf
    &
    & \downarrow Tf
    \\
    c^{\prime}
    &
    & Sc^{\prime}
    & \rightarrow
    & Tc^{\prime}
\end{array}
$$

commutative.
