## 08.02 Principal ideal domains (P.I.D.s)


### Definition P.I.D.
* $R$,
    * integral domain

$R$ is said to be P.I.D. if every ideal $I$ is principal.

### Remark
* Porposition 1 in Seciton 8.2 proves every Euclidean Domain is a P.I.D.
