## 02-01. Definition and examples

### Definition. subgroup
* $G$,
    * group
* $H \subseteq G$,
    * subset

$H$ is said to be subgroup of $G$ if

* (i) closed under products
* (ii) closed under inverses.

We write $H \le G$.
