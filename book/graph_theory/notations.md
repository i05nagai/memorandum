# Appendix.

## Notations

- $c(G)$,
    - number of components in the graph $G$
- $c(\Sigma)$
    - Euler charactisti of surface $\Sigma$,
- $c(a)$
    - capacity or cost of arc in network
- $c(x, y)$,
    - least size of cut separating two given vertices
- $c(\mathcal{F}, G)$,
    - number of overs by family of graphs
