## 8.2


### 8.2.5 Weak convergence

#### Definition 8.33
* $$\{P_{n}\}$$,
    * Borel probablity measures on $\mathbb{R}^{n}$,
* $P$,
    * Borel probablity measures on $\mathbb{R}^{n}$,
* $$\{F_{n}\}$$,
    * CDF of $$\{P_{n}\}$$,
* $F$,
    * Borel probablity measures on $\mathbb{R}^{n}$,

$$\{P_{n}\}$$ is said to converge wealy to $P$ if 

$$
    F_{n}(x) \rightarrow F(x)
$$

where $x$ is all continuous points of $F$.
