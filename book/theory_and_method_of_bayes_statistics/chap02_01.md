## 2.1 Formal Relation between True and Model

#### Definition 1
* $W \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{d}$,

$W$ is said to be realizable by a statistical model $p(x \mid w)$ if
 
$$
    \exists W \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{d}
    \text{ s.t. }
    q(x) = p(x \mid w)
    .
$$

Otherwise, $q$ is unrealizable.
